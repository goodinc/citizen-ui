What is a Do?

A Do is a creative action anyone can take right now toward a better self and a better world. Keep it open-ended, so everyone can do it and add a Story.

One great example: Interact with One Stranger Today

What isn't a Do?

Do posts aren't for clicktivism, petitions, crowd funding, events or marketing. Have a post that's GOOD, but doesn't work as a Do? Share a Link instead.
