    I ride my bike to work every day. I live just outside of Los Angeles so it can be a bit hairy for the first 3 miles until I get to the bike path. My ride includes 3 miles of awesome bike path and just over 3 miles of bike lane. A total of 9.5 miles each way. I wish the bike lanes were better maintained but I am frankly just happy they are there. My first 3 miles needs a bike lane BADLY. Whittier Boulevard is a major street and its downright scary on the way home. Overall though, I love my commute and am much happier at work when I ride in versus driving in.
