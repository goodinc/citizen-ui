Hello! There are many ways to get in touch with GOOD. Please choose what works for you.

Customer Service

For questions about your membership or magazine subscription, please send an email to help@goodinc.com or call 323-556-6780. If you’re experiencing a technical issue with the website, please submit a support request or send an email to help@goodinc.com.

Editorial/Submissions

If you’re a writer, photographer, or illustrator, you can submit samples of your work or pitches to submissions@goodinc.com.

Video

If you’re a filmmaker and would like to share your work, e-mail video@goodinc.com or call 323-556-5842 to get in touch.

Advertising and Partnerships

To learn more about advertising with GOOD, click here. To get in touch directly with someone on our sales team, please contact advertise@goodinc.com or call 323-556-5870.

If you’re a nonprofit organization interested in working with GOOD, check out our FAQs on using good.is as an engagement tool and/or email activation@goodinc.com.

Accounts Payable

If you have any questions for Accounting call 323-556-6790 or email accounting@goodinc.com.

GOOD Maker

If you have questions related to GOOD Maker, check out our FAQs or email maker@goodinc.com. If your organization is interested in launching a Challenge on GOOD Maker, please email us at maker@goodinc.com with "Challenge Partner" in the subject line.

GOOD/Corps

You can learn more about GOOD/Corps at our website. To get in touch, please e-mail hello@goodcorps.com or call 323-904-3711.

General Inquiries

E-mail us at info@goodinc.com or call 323-556-6780.
